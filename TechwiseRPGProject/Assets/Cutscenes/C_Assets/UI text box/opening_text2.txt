THEIR EYES WIDEN IN HORROR!!! 
The sight the greets them shatters their spirit.
Their home, once a sanctuary of love and solace, 
lies ravaged and decimated by a merciless force. 
The smoldering ruins whisper tales of sorrow 
and loss, casting long shadows over the hero's heart. 
With clenched fists, the hero swears to 
unearth the truth, seek justice, and restore 
their shattered world from the grasp of darkness. 
For in the face of unspeakable devastation, 
a new legend is born. The journey of the hero 
has just begun, and destiny beckons them to 
reclaim what was taken, to find redemption 
amidst the ruins, and to herald a dawn of light 
in this unforgiving realm.
